What?

4Reel Fishing is a company dedicated to cleaning and making the waters a safe and clean place.
4Reel FIshing creates biodegradable lines that degrades faster than the standard fishing line. Did you know that the average fishing line does not deteriorate for hundreds of years?

Why?

I created this company because I am dedicated to maintaining a healthy, safe and clean environment. I enjoy fishing with my friends and family as well as by myself, but each year I notice more and more fishing lines in trees and even power lines.

Why Us?

We provide a competitive advantage by providing a high quality line that as well keeps the waters clean. 5% of profits are donated to nonprofit organizations such as The Ocean Conservancy and The Ocean Cleanup.

Our Tagets and Our Mission

Our target market is all fishermen. We want to support the cleanliness of the ocean and waters for competitive fishers and all those people who enjoy a nice day fishing on a lake or pond. Our mission is to maintain the natural environment and the health and safety of all animals in or out of the water.

Donations

Our company donates 5% of all profits to help save the ocean.
